WE’RE HERE FOR YOU

The YMCA of Middle Tennessee is part of the nation's leading nonprofit committed to strengthening communities in our region through youth development, healthy living and social responsibility.

Learn more about us.

LEARN GROW THRIVE

At the Y, we provide programs, services and initiatives designed to help everyone in our community to learn, grow and thrive. In the process, we enable children and teens to reach their potential, empower people of all ages to be healthier in spirit, mind and body and support neighbors in need.


YOUTH DEVELOPMENT

We believe the values and skills learned early on are vital building blocks for life. That's why we're committed to inspiring children and teens to realize their full potential by learning values and positive behaviors and exploring their unique talents and interests.

Learn more

HEALTHY LIVING

At the Y, we believe a community is strongest when everyone has the opportunity to live healthier in all areas of life—spirit, mind and body. That's why we're committed to providing access to the facilities, people and resources to improve our community's health and well-being.

Learn more

SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

We know that we can best have a meaningful, enduring positive impact on our community when we join hands together to serve others and support our neighbors in need. That's why the Y is committed to providing opportunities for individuals to give back, and to develop the civic partnerships and resources needed to address our most pressing community challenges.

ALWAYS HERE FOR OUR COMMUNITY

For more than 130 years, the YMCA of Middle Tennessee has been guided by its Christian mission and values to strengthen the foundations of our community and nurture the potential of the men, women and children who call the greater-Nashville area home. As a leading nonprofit dedicated to meeting community needs, we engage people across 12 counties, giving more than 329,000 of our neighbors—nearly half of them kids—the chance to learn, grow and thrive.
