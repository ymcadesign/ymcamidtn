HEALTHY STARTS HERE

We are part of the nation's leading nonprofit committed to strengthening communities in Middle Tennessee and southern Kentucky through youth development, healthy living and social responsibility.

The number of Tennesseans living in poverty has risen to 1,095,000 up 22% from 2005. The good news is our community has proven - from the floods of 2010 to our more recent time of economic uncertainty - it is more than willing to rise to the occasion and help those most in need.
